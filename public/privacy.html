Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

What we collect

When you use IronWork we collect:

  • Account information — email address and password (hashed)
  • Business profile — business name, owner name, phone number
  • Work data — work orders, change orders, invoices, and client details you create
  • Usage data — basic analytics to understand how the product is used

How we use it

We use your data solely to operate and improve IronWork. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-party services

We use the following sub-processors to run the service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • DocuSeal — e-signature processing

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion by emailing us.

Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@ironwork.app.

Changes

We may update this policy. We'll note the date of the last update at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@ironwork.app.
